\[
y = 4x^2 + 2ax + b

\[
b = \boxed{AB}a^2 + \boxed{C}a + \boxed{D}
\]

并且，表示该图形的二次函数是

\[
y = \boxed{E}x^2 - \boxed{F}ax + \boxed{G}
\]
