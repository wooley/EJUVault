# H23-2-II-1

内分点公式：

- \(AP:PB=k:2\Rightarrow P=\dfrac{2A+kB}{k+2}\)
\[
P\left(\frac{2}{k+2},\frac{k}{k+2}\right)
\]

- \(CQ:QD=k:2\Rightarrow Q=\dfrac{2C+kD}{k+2}\)
\[
Q\left(\frac{6}{k+2},\frac{2k}{k+2}\right)
\]

因此
\[
\overrightarrow{PQ}=(x,y)=\left(\frac{4}{k+2},\frac{k}{k+2}\right)=\frac1{k+2}(4,k)
\]
\[
x+2y=\frac{4+2k}{k+2}=2
\]

令 \(y=\dfrac{k}{k+2}\Rightarrow x=2(1-y)\)，则
\[
PQ^2=x^2+y^2=4(1-y)^2+y^2=5y^2-8y+4
\]
最小值在
\[
y=\frac{8}{2\cdot 5}=\frac45
\]
\[
PQ^2=\frac45\Rightarrow PQ=\frac{2}{\sqrt5}=\frac{2\sqrt5}{5}
\]
并且
\[
\frac{k}{k+2}=\frac45\Rightarrow k=8
\]

**填空：**  
A=2, B=2, C=2, D=2, E=2, F=4, G=2, H=5, I=8, J=4, K=4, L=5, M=2, N=5, O=5, P=8
